Building an AI-Powered Mock Interview Platform
The Problem
Have you ever frozen during a technical interview — even though you knew the answer?
You studied for weeks. You can solve Coding problems at home. You can explain React hooks to your friends. But the moment an interviewer says "Tell me about yourself," your mind goes blank. 

The Action Gap
Knowing answers ≠ Performing in interviews

Watching Virat Kohli bat on TV won't make you hit a six. You need to face the ball yourself. Reading about system design won't prepare you for explaining it live to a senior engineer. The gap between knowledge and performance is real — and it only closes with practice under realistic conditions.

Traditional Solutions and Their Limitations
Approach	Limitation
Practice with friends	May not ask industry-relevant questions
Watch YouTube	You're not actually speaking
Read Q&A lists	Memorizing ≠ Explaining under pressure
Paid mock interviews	Expensive and limited availability
The core issue is clear: you need a way to practice speaking your answers out loud, get real-time feedback, and do it as many times as you want — without scheduling constraints or high costs.

The Solution: AI Powered Mock Interview Platform
An AI interviewer available 24/7 that:
- Asks relevant, role-specific questions
- Listens to your verbal answers
- Evaluates your code submissions
- Gives instant, detailed feedback

Instead of waiting days for a friend's availability or paying hundreds for a coaching session, you open your browser and start practicing immediately. The AI adapts questions to your resume, remembers context from earlier answers, and scores you across multiple dimensions.
