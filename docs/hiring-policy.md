# AgentHire Hiring Policy

## Evaluation principles

Candidates are evaluated only on evidence found in their resume: demonstrated skills,
total professional experience, education, and shipped projects. Required skills carry the
most weight in scoring, followed by preferred skills and experience relative to the
role's minimum. Skills are matched case-insensitively and common aliases (for example
"ReactJS" and "React") are treated as the same skill.

## Fairness rules

Hiring decisions must be traceable to the scoring breakdown and the shortlisting
thresholds in the evaluation specs. No candidate may be rejected for attributes
unrelated to the role requirements. Every automated decision is reviewed by a human
recruiter at the approval checkpoint before an interview invitation is sent.

## Experience policy

Experience below the role minimum reduces the experience portion of the score
proportionally rather than disqualifying the candidate outright. Strong skill coverage
can place a lower-experience candidate in the hold band for recruiter review.
